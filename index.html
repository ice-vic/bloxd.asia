üí° Pro Jumping Techniques

Diagonal Jumping

Use diagonal routes to maximize jump distance. Perfect for crossing wider gaps!

Sprint Jumping

Always sprint before jumping. Essential for gaps of 2+ blocks distance.

Edge Jumping

Jump from the very edge of blocks for maximum distance. Timing is crucial!
