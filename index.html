🎮 How to Play Bloxd.io

Getting Started: Simply click the play button above to launch the game directly in your browser. No downloads or installations required!

Basic Controls:

• Use WASD keys to move your character around the world

• Left-click to break blocks and right-click to place them

• Use the mouse to look around and navigate your view

• Press E to open your inventory and manage resources

• Use the number keys (1-9) to quickly select tools and blocks

Game Modes:

Choose from various game modes including Creative Mode for unlimited building, Survival Mode for resource management challenges, and Multiplayer Mode for collaborative or competitive play with other players.

Pro Tips:

• Start by gathering basic resources like wood and stone

• Build a shelter before nightfall in Survival Mode

• Experiment with different block combinations for unique designs

• Join multiplayer servers to learn from experienced players
